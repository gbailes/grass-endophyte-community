Sampling of native grass endophyte communities in endangered prairies of the Pacific Northwest, USA

Using culture-free, full-community DNA sequencing, we investigated the diversity, composition, and structure of full fungal foliar endophyte communities in two species of native, cool-season bunchgrasses. 

Drivers of grass endophyte communities in prairies of the Pacific Northwest, USA
